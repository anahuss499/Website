## Next Steps: Get Your Site Indexed

### **Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console** (MOST IMPORTANT)

1. **Go to**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add property**: Enter `https://www.mahmoodmasjid.com`
4. **Verify ownership** (choose one method):
   - HTML file upload (easiest)
   - Add meta tag to homepage
   - DNS verification
5. **Submit sitemap**:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `https://www.mahmoodmasjid.com/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

### **Step 2: Request Indexing**

In Google Search Console:
1. Go to **URL Inspection** tool
2. Enter: `https://www.mahmoodmasjid.com`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Repeat for important pages:
   - `/quran.html`
   - `/prayer-calendar.html`
   - `/azkar.html`
   - `/durood.html`

### **Step 3: Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools**

1. **Go to**: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Add your website
4. Submit sitemap: `https://www.mahmoodmasjid.com/sitemap.xml`
