From Dream to Reality

Mahmood Masjid — a center of worship, learning and community service in Gujrat. Every noble work begins with a pure intention; our mosque is the living result of that intention and the community's dedication.

Mosque Transformation

See the journey from our beloved old mosque to the beautiful new design that will serve our community for generations to come.

Old Mosque (1980s)

Built by Haji Noor Ilahi with pure intention and dedication, this mosque has served our community faithfully for decades. Though its structure has become fragile over time, its spiritual legacy remains strong.

New Mosque Design (2025)

A modern, safe, and spacious design that honors our heritage while embracing the future. With enhanced facilities for prayer, education, and community gatherings, the new mosque will be a beacon of faith and unity for generations.

Our Community & Beliefs

We are a community rooted in the Ahl as‑Sunnah wal‑Jama'ah tradition. Our mission is to serve the spiritual and educational needs of our neighbors through prayer, learning, and service.

Founding & History

Mahmood Masjid was founded out of the sincere intention of Haji Noor Ilahi. In the 1980s he vowed that if God granted him the means, he would build a house of worship. Through perseverance and God’s blessings he later funded and helped build the mosque.

The Name

The mosque is named Mahmood Masjid in honor of the founder’s grandfather, Mahmood, as a fulfilment of a family wish and a legacy of devotion.

Services

  • Five daily prayers and Jumu'ah
  • Religious education and Qur'an classes
  • Community outreach, charity, and support services

Current Condition & Reconstruction

The building has aged and requires careful rebuilding and renovation to ensure safety and sustained use. Plans are underway to restore and strengthen the mosque so it may continue serving generations to come.
